About us

We are a family-run, Limerick-based company, in business for the past 10 years. We specialise in raw, organic honey production by traditional methods, using skills passed down through generations of beekeepers. You can buy our produce on site. We also supply local shops with honey, hampers and handmade beeswax candles.

About our bees

There are 99 species of bee in this country. Our honey is produced by the only
honey-producing bee that is native to Ireland, the Irish Black Bee (Apis Melifera melifera). Also known as the European Dark Bee, Ireland has one of the largest populations of these bees left in the world. For more information on these magnificant creatures, go to the
Native Irish Honey Bee Society's website.

About our honey

Our raw, organic, local honey tastes wonderful drizzled on freshly made bread and porridge. It can also be used to sweeten hot beverages.
